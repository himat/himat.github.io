These are the values, principles, and beliefs of my life that my subconscious mind has repeated so often that my conscious mind could finally notice and codify them.

  • Creation is everything. I must always be changing the things around me, never preserving them.
  • I would die for my friends.
  • Love is the most powerful emotion in the world - I will promote love.
  • Stagnation is death.
  • Behavior = f(character, environment). Corollary: Don't immediately assume the worst in others.
  • Without me actively doing/starting anything, nothing will move forward.
  • I like people who think. Unfortunately, “Many people would rather die than think; in fact, many do." ~ Bertrand Russell.
  • I like people, in general. Talking with, noticing, and interacting with all people is one of the best parts of my day and life.
  • No one is a leader, but everyone has the option to make leadership decisions. Every day, we are given an inordinate number of opportunities to make such decisions.
  • I usually assume the best in others.
  • I like playing perfect information games. But, I also like playing imperfect information games, so that I can turn them into perfect ones.
  • I must always change the environment around me, this is how magic is created.
  • We only have one life to live and we cannot do everything. This is why I want to hear the experiences of others. We can pseudo-live a million lives, and learn part of what they learned.
  • First impressions are always wrong. People always impress you more than you would have thought.
  • Globalization increases the world average, but doesn't move the min.
  • Most people are too timid to take matters into their own hands.
  • Someone, such as governments, must fund basic research (otherwise the positive externalities don't manifest).
  • There's no reason to be sad for an extended amount of time, we must mourn when mourning is due, but for all else, what's done is done.
  • If you have nothing to lose from trying something, then just do it.
  • We are able to control our emotions and feelings to a much greater degree than we humans normally think we can.
  • Every day, I must push this one body I am given.
  • Seamless. In the ideal form, everything should be seamless. This is my guiding design principle.
  • If you don't write it down, it doesn't exist.
