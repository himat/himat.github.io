Questions I'm still thinking about:

  • Should developing countries go straight to using renewable energy sources, and leapfrog coal and other non-renewables?
  • How do I balance the enormous amount of information I could be consuming around me at any given moment with actually actioning on that information? (i.e. How do I re-balance my information-action ratio?)
  • It is very hard to change a city. But, I believe there are transportation methods we haven't conceived of yet that will benefit from future restructurings of our cities. So, how can we make physical infrastructure more fluid?
  • How do we reconcile making policy decisions that are good overall for one group, say the environment, but which may hurt a large part of another group?
  • Can we make a general framework for growing a given country, from undeveloped to developed?
  • I believe most people do not achieve nor even think about their full potential. How can I help more people better leverage themselves and their interests?
